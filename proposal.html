Wonderlust

Travel for the curious

Wonderlust is a website dedicated to travel and travel ideas for people looking for unique adventures. I chose this topic because I enjoy traveling and seeing new places.

Wonderlust Goals

Wonderlust will utilize advertising as a means of monetization. We will utilize both ad networks and direct sales for ad buyers.

Current goals will focus on growth with unique visitors and pageviews. This will transition to user growth with profiles, allowing for users to provide user generated content and continue to produce more content for viewers.

Growth will be focused on SEO, social and email marketing.

Wonderlust will provide users a strong sense of community for travelers in order to provide value to register. Users will be travelers with unique places to visit with content shared of these places.

Audience

Our audience demographic will be subject to various demographics, as many people can have a desire to travel at different stages in life. In order to capture our audience, we will utilize social media custom audiences and metrics from paid advertising and branding to differentiate different age ranges and associations within each age range. We will then focus on microtargeting these audiences for optimal effectiveness for each specific set of users that have a high conversion rate for becoming a user.

Client

The client will intially be myself, to attempt to monetize the site and gain a strong user base. After a user base has grown and continues to produce user generated content, the site would look for a travel or hospitality company for acquisition in order to implement native adberitising within our community to promote their brand.
